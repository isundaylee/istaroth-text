# Talk Dialog

Traveler: Tell me about the bitter...
Arouet: Every customer has different tastes in coffee, and I shall satisfy them as I am able.
Arouet: But if someone were to order a coffee without a trace of bitterness, well, then, my abilities would be quite limited.
Arouet: In such circumstances, I would ask them to go to the Palais Mermonia or Salon Caritat, and speak to the first lady or gentleman they meet.
