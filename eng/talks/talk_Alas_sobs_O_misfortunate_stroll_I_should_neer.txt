# Talk Dialog

Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): Alas! *sobs* O misfortunate stroll! I should ne'er have suggested thee.
Traveler: What happened?
Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): A bosom friend and I did'st walk about these parts, but by hilichurls were set upon...
Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): They have taken him. My friend was taken in my defense!
Traveler: Where have the hilichurls gone?
Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): I did not see them clearly, but I am sure they are on this island still. *sobs* Oh, what is to be done?
