# Seed Dispensary

A special container obtained from Madame Ping. According to her, this dispensary has the power to "generate seeds"... That is to say, it can create the seeds of various plants according to their special traits, which can then be planted within the appropriate fields within your Serenitea Pot.
Plants available for planting: Silk Flower, Sweet Flower, Cecilia, Glaze Lily, Windwheel Aster, Qingxin, Violetgrass, Valberry, Small Lamp Grass, Jueyun Chili, Carrot, Radish, Mint, Mushroom, Naku Weed, Horsetail, Snapdragon, Lotus Head, Calla Lily, Seagrass, Sea Ganoderma, Harra Fruit, Zaytun Peach, Sumeru Rose, Pluie Lotus, Marcotte, Lumidouce Bell, Rainbow Rose, Grainfruit, Cacahuatl, Saurian Claw Succulent, Quenepa Berry, Brilliant Chrysanthemum, Sprayfeather Gill, Glowing Hornshroom, Skysplit Gembloom.
