# Talk Dialog

Traveler: I'd like to ask about something.
Blanche: I'm listening.
Traveler: What makes Mondstadt General Goods special?
Blanche: Our shop may be small, but we do have a warehouse in Liyue Harbor.
Blanche: We take pride in having the largest catalog of items and the fastest shipping in all of Mondstadt.
Traveler: What is Mondstadt known for?
Blanche: Mondstadt is a city known for its wines. Taverns are where you'd want to go.
Blanche: Most dwellers here go to one of the taverns for a drink after a hard day's work.
Blanche: You can meet all sorts of people in the taverns at night.
