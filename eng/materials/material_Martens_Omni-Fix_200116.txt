# Martens' Omni-Fix

Specialized refinement material for "Fleuve Cendre Ferryman."
Not only can it prevent oxidation and erosion by forming an isolation layer on certain materials, but it also helps lubricate, clean, and polish. It was once all the rage in Fontaine. In the advertisements back then, a legendary figure from the Fleuve Cendre used it to maintain the famous curved copper tube.
