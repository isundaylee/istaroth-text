# Talk Dialog

Traveler: Didn't sleep well?
Ruying: Ah, yes.
Ruying: This inn is great. The owner is friendly, and the service is good. We foreign merchants like staying here.
Ruying: But there is one problem: the noise insulation is bad.
Ruying: I could hear the buzz of conversation next door while lying in bed at night.
Ruying: And I don't know if the man and woman next door were speaking in a dialect or something... I could understand the words but not the meaning.
