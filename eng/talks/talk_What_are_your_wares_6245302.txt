# Talk Dialog

Traveler: What are your wares?
Tai: Interested, are we? Well then, allow me to tell you a little something—
Tai: Look at this porcelain bottle, if you will. It may look plain, but it is a true relic, perhaps worth as much as half of Chihu Rock!
Tai: And this censer — legend has it that it has offered incense to Rex Lapis himself for a thousand years, and has become filled with adeptal energy in the process!
Tai: And have I mentioned this teapot? Why, it was once used by Rex Lapis himself. You will never find another like it...
