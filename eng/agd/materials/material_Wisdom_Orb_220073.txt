# Wisdom Orb

A crucial item provided by the organizer of the Nilotpala Cup Beast Tamers Tournament. Use it to capture, accommodate, and command intelligent Fungi. Word has it that it was invented by a researcher named Kautilya. Only those who can wield this item skillfully are qualified to participate in the Nilotpala Cup Beast Tamers Tournament. That said, you cannot help but feel that some of this item's functions create a strong sense of déjà vu...
