# Talk Dialog

Message: "Personally, I think artistic trends in today's Fontaine should progress in a more realistic direction, and let the beauty and homeliness that we see with our own two eyes bloom upon the canvas."
Message: "The beauty and homeliness we see with our own two eyes? Are you from the Kamera Club?"
Another Person's Message: "How dare you!? I am not! I am a painter! A painter!"
Message: "A Kamera is but a cold, unfeeling machine! The pictures it takes cannot be called art! True art requires thinking and soul!"
