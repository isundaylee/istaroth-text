# Talk Dialog

Traveler: Tell me about yourself...
Tubby: I do, in fact, have an adeptus name, it's just awfully difficult to remember... What was it again... Ah yes, "Rain Calmer."
Tubby: There was once a time of unending battle, when the land was swept by great storms, and neither the sun nor moon would shine.
Tubby: Ping and our old friends longed for days of sunshine, and thus, they gave me this name.
Tubby: That is why I always take on the color of the blue sky, no matter what form I take when presenting myself to others.
