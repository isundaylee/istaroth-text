# Talk Dialog

Traveler: Tell us about your relationship to this castle...
Monsieur Os: You've guessed it already, I reckon. In the past, when I could still change my form freely, he... and I, and many of our companions dwelled here.
Monsieur Os: In those days, a new nation had just been born, and as far as we were concerned, the possibilities were yet endless.
Monsieur Os: Here, we organized the classics, researching music theory and magic, in the hopes that we might tame this perilous power and use it to open up a brighter age.
Monsieur Os: But... These possibilities would quickly become lost to us, and I alone remained...
Traveler: Monsieur Os...
Monsieur Os: It's fine. These things happened a long time ago.
