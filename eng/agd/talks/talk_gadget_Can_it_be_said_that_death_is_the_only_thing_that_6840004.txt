# Talk Dialog

Message: "Can it be said that death is the only thing that is truly fair?"
Another Person's Message: "I do not think so. Fairness should be interpreted as a set of principles or objective standards. But death is merely a phenomenon, and not a standard, so it cannot be said to be fair."
Owner's Reply: "Our shop welcomes all philosophical discussions except those about 'death.' Sorry for any inconvenience and thank you for your understanding."
Message: "The topic of 'death' is admittedly grim, and considered inauspicious, but it is ultimately an unavoidable topic that we all must face."
Owner's Reply: "Our shop does not welcome any unavoidable philosophical discussions. Thank you for your understanding."
