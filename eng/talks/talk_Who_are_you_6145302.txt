# Talk Dialog

Traveler: Who are you?
Susie: I am just like you, one that is blessed by Barbatos, the Anemo Archon.
Susie: Shouldn't we show our gratitude to Barbatos for the wind and warmth?
Traveler: Wind of Mondstadt?
Susie: Mondstadt is a city blessed by the Anemo Archon Barbatos.
Susie: The wind blowing on Mondstadt is Barbatos' blessing.
