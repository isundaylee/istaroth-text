Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): The Maison Gardiennage discovered that the identities they chose to use were those of prisoners currently serving sentences within the Fortress of Meropide. The two suspects were arrested and questioned, and during the course of the questioning, their other criminal activity was uncovered.
Unknown Role (TALK_ROLE_GADGET): The final verdict: The Iudex and the Oratrice Mecanique d'Analyse Cardinale judge that the two defendants are guilty of multiple counts of the crime of burglary, and will be sent to the Fortress of Meropide to serve their sentences.
