# Illusory Leafcoil

A core formed from tightly coiled leaves, the remnant of the monster known as the Tenebrous Mimiflora after being vanquished.
It may appear lifeless and dull on the surface, yet a faint, ominous power can still be sensed pulsing within.
People often wonder where the memories that such creatures drew from the earth go when they are slain. Perhaps the answer lies within this core...
